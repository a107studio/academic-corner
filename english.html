终末之诗(End Poem)

Posted by 107English

“终末之诗（End Poem）”是基于文本的一段消息，会在玩家进入返回传送门时出现。

I see the player you mean.
[Playername]?
Yes. Take care. It has reached a higher level now. It can read our thoughts.
